Certified Assessments — Long Island

Official benchmarks.
Verified by a coach.

Athletes near Long Island can visit Victory Sports Performance in Northport, NY for an in-person, coach-verified assessment session. Certified data carries a distinct badge in the app and greater accuracy weight in your Edge Score.

What “Certified” Means

Self-reported data is useful.
Certified data is definitive.

Most athletes log benchmarks themselves — which is valuable and how the majority of Edge Score data is collected. But self-reported numbers can carry uncertainty: different surfaces, equipment, conditions, or technique cues. A certified assessment removes that variability.

●

Standardized protocol

Every assessment follows the same warmup, surface, equipment, and measurement method so your numbers are comparable across athletes.

●

Coach-verified and signed off

A Victory Sports Performance coach observes, measures, and certifies each result. The numbers go into the app directly — no manual entry by the athlete.

●

Distinct badge in-app

Certified benchmarks show a verification mark in your dashboard. Anyone viewing your profile — including trainers — can see which data is certified vs. self-reported.

●

Higher accuracy weight

Your Edge Score calculation applies a higher confidence weighting to certified benchmarks. The more you certify, the more precisely your score reflects your actual athletic capability.

What We Test

Six athletic dimensions. One session.

A standard certified assessment covers all six Edge Score dimensions. Individual dimension assessments are also available if you want to re-certify a specific area after a training cycle.

01

Speed

10-yard dash, 40-yard dash, and flying 10 splits. Timed with hand and, where available, electronic timing.

02

Agility

Pro-agility (5-10-5) and T-drill. Scored for both time and positional accuracy to reward clean movement, not just raw speed.

03

Strength

Push-up max reps, squat hold time, and grip strength dynamometer reading. Normalized for age and bodyweight.

04

Power

Vertical jump (reach method), broad jump, and medicine ball overhead throw. Measured to the inch.

05

Endurance

Beep test (20m shuttle) or timed 400m run based on athlete age and sport profile. VO2 max estimated from result.

06

Recovery

Resting heart rate, HRV reading (where available), and self-reported sleep and soreness during the assessment window.
