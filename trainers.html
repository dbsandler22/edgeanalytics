For Trainers & Coaches

Coach smarter.
Track progress. Earn more.

Edge Athletics gives you the data to show what your coaching does, the tools to stay connected between sessions, and early-partner compensation for being part of a platform that works because you do.

Why Trainers Use Edge

Everything you need to run a smarter program.

Objective Progress Tracking

Stop relying on subjective impressions. Edge gives you and your athletes real numbers across six athletic dimensions — updated as they train. Data you can point to. Results you can defend.

Credibility with Parents

Parents want to see the value of what they are investing in. Edge gives you shareable, objective progress reports that make your impact visible — building trust and strengthening retention.

Visibility into Consistency

Know who is putting in the work between sessions and who is not. Your dashboard shows engagement, completion trends, and progress — so you can coach proactively, not reactively.

Workflow Support

Foundational programming inside Edge reinforces your work between sessions. Athletes follow structured routines that build the athletic base your sport-specific training depends on.

Role Clarity

Edge supports your coaching. It does not replace it.

Sport coaches are irreplaceable. The tactical knowledge, the relationships, the session-by-session adjustments — that is your expertise. Edge is not trying to automate that.

What Edge does is build the athletic foundation beneath your coaching — speed, strength, agility, power, endurance, recovery — so that when athletes show up for your sessions, they are more prepared, more durable, and more able to absorb and apply what you teach. Better athletes make better coaches look even better.

Early Partner Model

Earnings & Pricing (Early Partner Model)

Transparent Economics

You implement it. We compensate you for it.

Edge is free for trainers to join. Athlete families pay a monthly subscription. Early partner coaches receive ongoing compensation for rolling out the platform and actively supporting athlete engagement — not as a referral, but as aligned incentive for doing the work of implementation.

early partner coach compensation

Example: 25 active athletes = $49.75/month in ongoing support compensation. That is recurring — as long as athletes stay active on the platform.

Compensation begins after the first full month of an athlete's active participation. It is tied to ongoing engagement — not a one-time signup. This keeps your incentive aligned with athlete outcomes, which is exactly where it should be.
