Resources

Everything you need
to get started.

Materials for trainers rolling out Edge Athletics and families getting their athlete onboarded. Resources are distributed to early partners before the general launch.

Trainer Toolkit

Built to make your rollout easy.

Early trainer partners receive a complete onboarding toolkit before they go live with their athletes. Every resource below is included in the early access package.

📄
PDF Guide

Trainer Rollout Guide

Step-by-step instructions for setting up your trainer account, generating your roster invite code, and walking athletes through registration. Covers the first 30 days in detail.

Available to early partners at onboarding
💬
Talk Track

Athlete & Parent Introduction Script

A ready-to-use conversation guide for introducing Edge Athletics to athletes and parents in a session or group setting. Covers what it is, why it matters, and how to join.

Available to early partners at onboarding
💌
Email Template

Parent Announcement Email

A pre-written email template you can send to parents announcing Edge Athletics and prompting athletes to register with your trainer code. Personalize with your name and training program details.

Available to early partners at onboarding
📊
Reference Sheet

Dashboard Quick-Reference Card

A one-page summary of the trainer dashboard: what each metric means, how to read dimension breakdowns, and how to generate a parent progress report. Print-friendly format.

Available to early partners at onboarding
🎓
Walkthrough

Onboarding Video Walkthrough

A short screen-share video walking through every section of the trainer dashboard, how to track athlete progress, and how to read Edge Score breakdowns. Paired with the PDF guide.

Video in production — available at launch

How to get these resources

Trainer toolkit materials are sent directly to early partner trainers during the onboarding walkthrough. They are not publicly downloadable — this ensures each trainer is set up correctly before going live with athletes.

Express your interest to get on the early partner list and receive priority access.

Family Guide

Simple resources for parents and athletes.

Families receive onboarding materials when they join the platform. Below is a preview of what’s included.

📚
Welcome Guide

Family Getting Started Guide

Everything a parent and athlete need to know to set up their account, log their first benchmarks, and start building their Edge Score. Written for non-technical users.

Available to waitlist families at launch
🎯
Explainer

Understanding Your Edge Score

A plain-language breakdown of how the Edge Score is calculated, what each of the six dimensions represents, and how to interpret your athlete’s progress over time.

Available to waitlist families at launch
✅
Checklist

Benchmark Logging Checklist

A printable checklist of the benchmarks athletes can self-report at home, at school, or in practice — with instructions for each test and what equipment (if any) is needed.

Available to waitlist families at launch
🔒
Privacy Overview

What Data We Collect & Why

A family-readable summary of our data practices: what information is collected, who can see it, how it is protected, and what controls parents have over their athlete’s data.
