For Parents & Athletes

Progress you can see.
Development that lasts.

Edge Athletics helps families understand what is actually happening with their athlete's development — in plain numbers, across the skills that matter in every sport.

Why Families Use Edge

More than practice. More than games.

Development happens between the lines, not just on the field. Edge gives you the visibility to see it.

Visible Progress

See your athlete's development tracked week by week across six foundational dimensions: strength, speed, agility, power, endurance, and recovery. Not just game stats — actual athletic development data.

Consistency Tracking

Development is built between sessions, not just during them. Edge tracks workout consistency and shows the connection between effort and improvement over time.

Balanced Athletic Development

Edge focuses on the foundational qualities that support performance in any sport — and that help athletes stay healthy and available. Not sport-specific technique — the athletic foundation underneath all of it.

The Edge Score

A single composite score that reflects your athlete's overall athletic development across all six dimensions. It updates as they train and as benchmarks are logged. It is a guide to growth, not a label.

Coach-Supported Development

If your athlete works with a trainer or coach, Edge connects seamlessly. Coaches get visibility into progress between sessions, which means better conversations, better adjustments, and better outcomes for your athlete.

How Benchmarks Work

Self-Reported vs Certified Benchmarks

We believe in transparency. Edge supports two types of benchmark data — and they are always clearly labeled.

Self-Reported

Logged in the App

Athletes log their own benchmarks inside the app from wherever they are. This data is the foundation of the Edge Score and is labeled as self-reported in the platform. It is how most athletes will track day-to-day progress.

Certified

Verified at VSP (Long Island)

Athletes who want an official accuracy layer can book an in-person session at Victory Sports Performance in Northport, NY. A trained coach runs standardized assessments and records the results. Certified benchmarks are marked distinctly in the app and carry greater statistical weight.

Coaches who use Edge may receive compensation for their role.

When a trainer or coach introduces Edge to athletes and actively supports their participation over time, they may receive ongoing compensation as part of the platform model. This is how we align everyone's interests — when athletes stay active on the platform, their coaches benefit too.

We disclose this because we think you deserve to know. The goal is aligned incentives: coaches are rewarded for implementation and athlete engagement, not simply for signing people up. If you have questions about this, feel free to reach out directly.
