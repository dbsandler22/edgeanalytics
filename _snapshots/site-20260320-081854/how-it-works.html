How It Works

Simple by design.
Powerful by data.

See the step-by-step flow for families and trainers. Toggle between them below.

01

Join or get invited

Create your account on the Edge Athletics app, or join via an invite code from your trainer or coach. Getting started takes a few minutes.

02

Log workouts and benchmarks

Track your training sessions and record performance benchmarks across six athletic dimensions: strength, speed, agility, power, endurance, and recovery. The more you log, the more accurate your Edge Score becomes.

03

See your Edge Score and dimension breakdown

Your Edge Score updates as you train. Dive into the breakdown to see where you are strongest and where you have the most room to grow. Progress is tracked over time so you can see the arc of your development.

04

Engage with contextual leaderboards

Compare your progress against relevant peers — filtered by age group, sport, team, or geography. The goal is to motivate progress, not create ranking pressure. Leaderboards are opt-in and contextual.

05

Certify your benchmarks Optional — Long Island

Athletes near Long Island can visit Victory Sports Performance in Northport, NY for an official, coach-verified assessment. Certified benchmarks are marked distinctly in the app and carry greater accuracy weight in your Edge Score.

01

Express interest and get onboarded

Fill out the trainer interest form. Early partners receive priority setup and a dedicated onboarding walkthrough before the general launch. We will walk you through everything you need to know.

02

Set up your roster

Receive your unique trainer code. Athletes join the platform and register with your code — linking them to your dashboard so you can see their progress, engagement, and active status in one place.

03

Assign programming and track progress

Athletes follow foundational athletic programs inside the app. You can see who is completing sessions, tracking benchmarks, and progressing across dimensions — visibility that extends far beyond your in-person hours.

04

Share progress data with parents

Your dashboard gives you shareable, objective progress reports. Use them in parent conversations to demonstrate your coaching impact with real data — not just anecdotes. Builds trust. Drives retention.

05

Earn ongoing compensation

After an athlete's first full month of active participation, you receive $1.99 per active athlete per month. This is not a one-time bonus — it recurs as long as athletes stay active. This compensates you for implementing Edge and actively supporting athlete engagement over time.
