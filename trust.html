Trust, Safety & Data

How we handle
your athlete’s data.

A plain-language explanation of what Edge Athletics collects, who can see it, how it is protected, and what controls parents have. No legal jargon here — that is what the Privacy Policy is for.

Our Commitments

Four things we commit to, without exception.

🔒

Athlete data is private by default

Athlete profiles, scores, and benchmarks are private by default. Only the athlete and their parent or guardian can see the full profile. No data is shared publicly without explicit opt-in.

🚫

We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade athlete personal data to any third party, including advertisers, data brokers, or recruiting services. Your athlete’s performance data is not a commercial product.

👤

Parents are in control

Parents and guardians have full control over their athlete’s account, data visibility, and opt-in settings including leaderboards. Parental consent is required for athletes under 13.

✅

Leaderboards are always opt-in

Athletes are never placed in visible rankings or peer comparisons without explicit consent. Contextual leaderboards are designed to motivate progress, not create social pressure or public rankings.

Data Collection

What we collect and why.

Account information

Name, email address, and date of birth (to determine age group normalization). For athletes under 13, parent or guardian email is required and parental consent is obtained before account activation.

Athletic performance data

Benchmark measurements across six athletic dimensions (speed, agility, strength, power, endurance, recovery). This is the core of what Edge Athletics tracks and is entirely the point of the platform. This data is used to calculate your Edge Score and is never shared with third parties.

Training activity logs

Session completion records: when athletes log a workout or benchmark session. Used to track engagement, calculate active status, and inform the trainer dashboard. Not shared publicly.

Trainer-athlete relationships

If an athlete joins using a trainer’s invite code, their progress data is visible to that trainer on the trainer dashboard. This is an intentional, consented relationship — the functional equivalent of a coach watching an athlete train.

What we do NOT collect

We do not collect location data, financial information, school records, medical information, or any data not directly related to athletic performance tracking. We do not track athletes across other websites or services.

Children’s Privacy

Special protections for athletes under 13.

Edge Athletics serves youth athletes, and we take children’s privacy seriously. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Parental consent required

Athletes under 13 cannot create or activate an account without verified parental consent. Registration requires a parent or guardian email, and the account is not activated until a consent confirmation step is completed by the parent.

Parent account controls

Parents can review all data collected about their athlete, update or correct information, request deletion of their athlete’s data, and opt-in or opt-out of leaderboard participation at any time. Contact hello@trainwithedge.com to exercise any of these rights.

No advertising to children

We do not serve advertisements to athlete accounts, collect data for advertising purposes, or permit third-party advertising on the platform. Athletes under 13 are never targeted by any marketing or personalization system based on their data.

Security

How data is protected.

Encrypted in transit and at rest

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using TLS. Stored data is encrypted at rest. Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Minimal data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service. If an account is deleted, associated performance data is purged from active systems within 30 days. Backups are rotated on a defined schedule.

Third-party services

We use a small number of third-party services to operate the platform (hosting, email delivery, analytics). These services are selected based on their privacy and security track record and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. A complete list is available in the Privacy Policy.

Questions about your data?

We are committed to answering data and privacy questions promptly. Reach out directly.

hello@trainwithedge.com

For formal legal requests, see the Privacy Policy for contact instructions.
