How Edge Works

01

Athletes Complete Weekly
Edge Challenges

Athletes complete expert-led Edge challenges designed to measure and develop critical athletic qualities like speed, agility, coordination, strength, and endurance.

Expert-led sports-specific skill work is also available.

02

Edge Builds A
Complete Athletic Score

Athletes log performance data, and Edge uses it to build a complete picture of athletic development over time.

The result is an Edge Score that tracks progress, benchmarks against other athletes, identifies gaps, and highlights what to work on next.

03

Trainers Track Progress And
Earn Monthly

Trainers can see athlete activity and development trends.

As athletes stay active, trainers earn a share of subscription revenue, creating recurring monthly income.

Participation is free for trainers and coaches.

Athletic programming developed by Victory Sports Performance (VSP).

See Progress From Every Angle

Athletes can track their Edge Score, benchmark performance by sport, age, and gender, and watch their athletic profile become more complete over time. Coaches and trainers get a clear view of activity, engagement, and development across the roster.

How Edge Complements Coaching Sessions

Trainers coach the sport. Edge helps measure and develop the athletic foundation underneath it —
speed, strength, agility, coordination, mobility, and endurance.

More Effective Sessions

Athletes complete warm-ups and foundational strength work before training, so your time is spent on tactics and technique.

Healthier, More Available Athletes

Structured recovery sessions reduce fatigue and overuse risk, helping players show up consistently.

Stronger Athletic Foundation

Speed, strength, coordination, flexibility, and endurance are developed systematically.

Visible Development

Objective data gives athletes, coaches, and families a shared view of progress over time.

Why Edge Exists

Young Athletes Need More Than Sport-Specific Reps.

Too often, athletes specialize before they have built the speed, strength, coordination, mobility, and endurance that support long-term growth and durability. Edge makes that foundation visible, measurable, and personalized.

01

Development Gap

Specializing early can narrow development.

Athletes can improve in one lane while underbuilding the full foundation that supports long-term growth.

02

Durability Risk

Missing foundational development can increase injury risk.

When the same patterns repeat too early, overuse risk rises and resilience can lag behind performance.

Source
03

Long-Term Payoff

Balanced development supports better outcomes.

When athletes build across dimensions, progress becomes healthier, clearer, and easier to sustain.

Edge helps athletes build a stronger all-around foundation for every sport they play.
